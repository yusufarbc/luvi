# AI Event at Marmara University

A special event where we discussed the philosophical and technical dimensions of technology with university students.

### Conference Topics:
- **Journey of Intelligence**: Comparison of artificial intelligence with biological intelligence.
- **Future Scenarios**: Sustainability of human existence in libertarian utopia scenarios.
- **Technological Measures**: Individual and corporate security measures in the developing AI world.
