We Met with IT Leaders at Istanbul Topkapı HolidayInn Hotel!

We discussed many current opportunities and risks from cyber security to artificial intelligence. In this widely attended event, which included Secude Bilişim, bringing strong brands in the cyber security field to our country, and Güney Bilişim, which has accomplished successful works in the same field, pleasant moments were also experienced.

This meeting, while reinforcing Luvi Bilişim's strong bonds with sector stakeholders, provided the opportunity to deeply analyze key topics such as the most current trends in the cyber security world and artificial intelligence integration. With the intense interest and valuable contributions of the participants, we continue to discuss the future of the cyber world starting from today.
