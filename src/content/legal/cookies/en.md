### Cookie Policy

As Luvi Bilişim, we use cookies to increase the performance of our website and improve the user experience.

### 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are saved on your device (computer, phone, etc.) when you visit a website.

### 2. Cookie Types
Only technical cookies that improve the user experience and analytical cookies that measure site performance (Google Analytics, etc.) are used on our site.

### 3. Control of Cookies
Users can block or delete the use of cookies via browser settings. However, disabling cookies may cause some features of the site not to work properly.

For your questions regarding the cookie policy, you can contact us at **bilgi@luvibilisim.com**.
