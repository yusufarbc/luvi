### Privacy Policy

As Luvi Bilişim, we prioritize the privacy of our users visiting our website above all else. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected during your use of our site and how this data is protected.

### 1. Collected Information
In case you fill out the contact form on our site, information such as the name, e-mail, phone number, and message content you share is collected in order to respond to your request and provide you with better service.

### 2. Data Security
All collected data is protected against unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure within the framework of Luvi Bilişim's high security standards (`EDR/XDR/SIEM`) and cyber security procedures. Your data is never shared with third parties, except for legal obligations.

### 3. Purpose of Use
Your data is used only for the following purposes:
*   Responding to your contact requests.
*   Providing information about our services.
*   Analyzing and improving the performance of our site.

### 4. Your Rights
Our users have the right to request the deletion or correction of the data they share at any time. For these requests, you can contact us at **bilgi@luvibilisim.com**.
