## Intelligent Defense Against Email Threats
Email remains the primary entry point for over 90% of cyber attacks. **Libraesva Email Security Gateway (ESG)**, backed by Luvi Bilişim, protects your organization against phishing, ransomware, and Business Email Compromise (BEC) with an AI-powered, multi-layered architecture.

---

## 1. URLSand™: Link-Based Protection
Clicking a link in an email can be the start of a disaster. **URLSand** technology scans all links in real-time and protects the user at the moment of clicking.

- **Link Rewriting:** All URLs are safely rewritten by Libraesva.
- **Time-of-Click Decision:** Even if a link was clean when the email arrived, Libraesva detects and blocks it instantly if it becomes "weaponized" at the time of clicking.
- **Page Analysis:** It doesn't just check lists; it actively scans the content of the destination page to detect phishing forms.

---

## 2. QuickSand™: File Sandboxing
Malicious code and macros hidden within Microsoft Office and PDF documents can bypass traditional antivirus solutions. **QuickSand** stops these threats by analyzing files before they even leave the gateway.

- **Active Content Analysis:** Deeply inspects macros, JavaScript code, and ActiveX components.
- **Document Sanitization:** Safely delivers the cleaned file to the user by removing potentially harmful active content from the document.
- **Speed and Privacy:** Files are not sent outside for analysis; they are analyzed within seconds on your own infrastructure (or in the cloud), preventing delays.

---

## 3. AI-Powered Phishing Protection
Libraesva doesn't just look at signatures; it analyzes the "intent" of the email.

- **Semantic Analysis:** Analyzes the content and tone of the email to detect human-written fraud attempts (Whaling/BEC).
- **Adaptive Trust Model:** Learns internal communication patterns and flags suspicious emails that deviate from these patterns in real-time.
- **DMARC/SPF/DKIM Analysis:** Implements advanced identity authentication checks against domain spoofing.

---

## 4. Flexible Deployment and Integration
Whether you use it on an on-premise virtual server or in a cloud architecture fully integrated with Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace.

- **Office 365 API Integration:** Easily add Libraesva's advanced engines as a second layer of defense on top of Microsoft's built-in security.
- **Encrypted Email:** Communicate securely by encrypting sensitive content end-to-end.
