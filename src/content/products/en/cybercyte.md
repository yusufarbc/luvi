# Cybercyte Threat Exposure Management

Cybercyte is an AI-powered platform that helps organizations manage cyber risks and detect hidden threats.

### Key Features:
- **Threat Visibility:** Identifies blind spots and potential infiltration points in your network.
- **GRC Compliance:** Streamlines compliance with standards like ISO 27001, NIST, and PCI.
- **Risk Analysis:** Provides cyber risk scoring for your assets.
- **Automated Response:** Ability to take quick action against detected threats.
