## Introduction: The Shift to Autonomous Security
Modern cyber threats operate at machine speed, requiring defense mechanisms that don't rely on human intervention for every step. **SentinelOne Singularity** is an industry-leading XDR platform that uses static and behavioral AI to stop attacks across the enterprise.

---

## 1. AI-Driven Prevention and Detection
Unlike legacy solutions, SentinelOne does not rely on daily signature updates.

- **Static AI:** Pre-execution analysis of files to identify malicious traits before they run.
- **Behavioral AI:** ActiveEDR® monitors every process on the endpoint, detecting fileless attacks, lateral movement, and malicious scripts in real-time.
- **Ranger® IoT:** Automatically discovers and secures unmanaged IoT and mobile devices on your network.

---

## 2. Storyline™: Automated Contextualization
SentinelOne's patented Storyline technology automatically links related events into a single, easy-to-understand narrative.

- **Instant Visibility:** View the complete lineage of an attack without manual log correlation.
- **Smart Response:** One-click rollback technology can restore a Windows device to its pre-infected state within seconds, neutralizing ransomware damage.

---

## 3. Singularity™ XDR (Extended Detection & Response)
Expanding visibility beyond the endpoint to include cloud, identity, and network data.

- **Data Lake Integration:** Ingest and analyze data from various sources (Firewalls, Cloud, Email) for a holistic security view.
- **Singularity Cloud:** Protecting containers, Kubernetes, and serverless workloads with the same AI-powered efficiency.
