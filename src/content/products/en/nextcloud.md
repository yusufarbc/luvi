## Your Data is Under Your Control
In cloud storage solutions, privacy and control are critical priorities. **Nextcloud Hub**, offered with Luvi Bilişim's expertise, is the most powerful content collaboration platform in the open-source world. Your data is not hosted on third-party cloud providers, but entirely on servers under your control (On-premise or Private Cloud).

---

## 1. Nextcloud Files: Advanced File Management
Access, sync, and share your files securely from anywhere (Desktop, Web, Mobile).

- **Universal Access:** Manage different storage areas such as FTP, SharePoint, Windows Network Drive, and S3 through a single interface.
- **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** Store files encrypted on your device so that even a server administrator cannot see them.
- **Smart File Locking:** Built-in file locking and versioning to prevent conflicts on the same file.

---

## 2. Nextcloud Office and Talk
Unify communication and productivity on a single platform.

- **Real-Time Collaboration:** Edit Microsoft Office documents through your browser simultaneously with your teammates.
- **Nextcloud Talk:** A video conferencing, chat, and webinar platform running on your completely secure and private server. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.
- **Groupware:** A full-featured alternative to Outlook with a corporate calendar, email client, and contact management.

---

## 3. AI and Automation (Nextcloud Flow)
Increase your efficiency by automating your business processes.

- **Nextcloud Flow:** Create scenarios such as automatic PDF conversion when a file is uploaded to a specific folder, sending an approval message to a manager, or automatic file tagging.
- **Local AI:** Text summarization and content generation with an AI engine running on your own server, without leaking your data to third parties (OpenAI, etc.).

---

## 4. Security and Compliance
Nextcloud offers an infrastructure fully compliant with the strictest privacy regulations (GDPR, KVKK).

- **File Access Control:** Restrict access to certain files based on the user's location, IP address, or device type.
- **Advanced Audit:** Track who accessed which file and when. Legally report all activities.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Protect user accounts with the highest security standards.
