## Introduction: The Evolution of Endpoint Defense
In an era of increasingly sophisticated cyber threats, traditional antivirus solutions are no longer sufficient. **Kaspersky Enterprise Security** provides a multi-layered defense strategy, combining world-class threat intelligence with automated response capabilities.

---

## 1. Kaspersky Endpoint Security (KESB)
The foundation of modern workplace protection, delivering next-generation prevention, detection, and remediation.

- **File, Web, and Mail Threat Protection:** Real-time scanning using heuristic analysis and cloud-based intelligence (KSN).
- **HIPS (Host Intrusion Prevention System):** Monitors application behavior and restricts access to critical system resources based on reputation.
- **Vulnerability & Patch Management:** Automatically identifies and patches software vulnerabilities to minimize the attack surface.

---

## 2. Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR)
Empowering security teams with advanced visibility and automated incident response.

- **Root Cause Analysis:** Visualizes the entire attack chain, from initial entry to lateral movement.
- **Automated Response:** Isolate hosts, terminate processes, or quarantine files across the network with a single click.
- **Threat Hunting:** Proactively search for Indicators of Compromise (IoCs) across all endpoints using advanced telemetry.

---

## 3. Managed Detection and Response (MDR)
24/7 expert monitoring and response for organizations requiring peak security posture.

- **Expert Monitoring:** Kaspersky SOC experts hunt for threats in your environment around the clock.
- **Guided Response:** Receive actionable recommendations and automated response scripts for complex incidents.
- **Compliance Ready:** Meet strict regulatory requirements with detailed reporting and audit logs.
