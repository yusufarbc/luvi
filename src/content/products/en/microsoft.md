## More Than Just Office Apps
In the modern business world, simply having Office applications is not enough. **Microsoft 365 Business Premium**, combined with Luvi Bilişim's cloud expertise, offers productivity tools alongside advanced cybersecurity and device management capabilities in a single package.

---

## 1. Microsoft Defender for Business
Specifically designed for small and medium-sized businesses, this endpoint security solution makes enterprise-grade protection accessible.

- **Next-Gen Antivirus:** Stops ransomware and advanced malware attacks before they even begin with its AI-powered engine.
- **EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response):** Monitors, analyzes, and automatically responds to attacks in real-time.
- **Vulnerability Management:** Narrows the attack surface by identifying missing updates and configuration errors on your devices.

---

## 2. Microsoft Intune: Device and Application Management
Securely manage the devices (Windows, macOS, iOS, Android) that access your corporate data, wherever your employees are.

- **Remote Management:** Automatically configure all settings via the cloud before sending a new computer to an employee (Autopilot).
- **Application Protection:** Prevent data leaks by blocking the copying of corporate data (e.g., attachments in Outlook) to personal applications.
- **Remote Wipe:** In case of loss or theft, wipe only corporate data from the device with a single click.

---

## 3. Microsoft Purview: Data Governance and Compliance
Know where your sensitive data (credit card info, ID numbers, etc.) is and protect it.

- **Information Protection (DLP):** Automatically blocks the unauthorized sharing of emails or documents containing sensitive data.
- **Sensitivity Labels:** Automatically encrypts content by adding labels like "Confidential" or "Internal" to your documents.
- **Compliance:** Ensure full compliance with legal regulations such as GDPR via data retention and deletion policies.

---

## 4. Advanced Identity Security (Entra ID)
Formerly known as Azure AD, **Microsoft Entra ID Premium** is the strongest line of defense against identity theft.

- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Prevents unauthorized access even if a password is stolen.
- **Conditional Access:** Grants system entry based on the user's location, device health status, and risk level.
