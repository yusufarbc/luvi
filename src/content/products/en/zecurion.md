## Introduction: Keeping Your Data Yours
The majority of data breaches stem from insider threats or human error rather than external attacks. **Zecurion Next-Gen DLP**, backed by Luvi Bilişim's expertise, monitors, classifies, and instantly prevents unauthorized data leaks in real-time.

---

## 1. 360-Degree Data Flow Control
Zecurion monitors over 100 channels through which data can exit your organization, all from a centralized console.

- **Channel Control:** Email (including SSL), Web traffic, USB drives, Printers, Cloud storage (Dropbox, OneDrive), and even instant messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, Skype).
- **Content Analysis:** Utilizes AI and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to detect sensitive data even within images.
- **Digital Fingerprinting:** "Fingerprints" your sensitive files, ensuring the data is recognized and protected even if the file name or format changes.

---

## 2. User Behavior Analytics (UBA)
Unusual behavior is often the first sign of a breach. Zecurion UBA learns the "normal" activity patterns for every employee.

- **Risk Scoring:** Creates a risk profile based on employee activities. If an employee suddenly copies large amounts of data or accesses the system at unusual hours, the system triggers an alert.
- **Emotional Profiling:** Analyzes the emotional tone of corporate communications using AI to identify high-risk groups or critical personnel experiencing a loss of motivation.

---

## 3. Revolutionary Screen Protection
With the world-first "Screen Photo Detector" technology, your data is safe even against physical photography.

- **Screen Photo Detection:** If a user tries to take a photo of the screen with a smartphone, Zecurion detects this via the webcam within seconds and automatically locks the screen.
- **Screen Watermarking:** Overlays sensitive windows with watermarks containing the username, IP, and timestamp to deter unauthorized photography or data smuggling.

---

## 4. Incident Management and Investigation
Automation tools that accelerate investigations for cybersecurity teams:

- **Investigation Workflow:** Track incidents by status (Open, Investigating, Closed) and assign responsible team members.
- **Screen Recording and Keylogging:** When a violation is detected, the system records the moments before and after the incident as video, providing forensic-grade evidence.
- **Staff Control:** Reports on employee productivity and working hours based on corporate standards.
