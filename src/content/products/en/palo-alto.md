## Cybersecurity Without Borders
Palo Alto Networks takes a "Platform" approach to cybersecurity, closing the gaps created by siloed solutions. Through the **Strata, Prisma, and Cortex** solutions provided by Luvi Bilişim, we redefine your organization's cyber defense with the power of AI and automation.

---

## 1. Strata™: The Foundation of Network Security (NGFW)
Protect your network against both known and unknown threats with the world's first "ML-Powered" (Machine Learning-Powered) next-generation firewalls.

- **PAN-OS®:** Provides consistent policy management across all form factors (Physical, Virtual, Container) using the same advanced operating system.
- **App-ID™:** Identifies and controls applications based on content, not just ports.
- **WildFire®:** Cloud-based malware analysis that announces and blocks new threats to the entire world within seconds.

---

## 2. Prisma™ Cloud: Cloud Security (CNAPP)
Secure your data and applications at every stage of your cloud journey (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).

- **Prisma Cloud:** A Cloud Native Application Protection Platform providing full visibility from code to runtime.
- **Prisma Access (SASE):** Offers your hybrid workforce secure and fast "Zero Trust" connectivity from anywhere.
- **Prisma SaaS:** Prevents data loss in cloud-based applications (Office 365, Salesforce, etc.).

---

## 3. Cortex™: Autonomous Security Operations (XDR)
Increase the speed at which your security teams respond to cyber incidents and automate manual processes.

- **Cortex XDR:** Automatically analyzes the root cause of attacks by integrating network, endpoint, and cloud data.
- **Cortex XSOAR:** Orchestrate your security operations with hundreds of integrations and ready-to-use "Playbooks."
- **Cortex XPANSE:** Constantly scans your organization's external-facing attack surface to identify vulnerable points.

---

## 4. Precision AI™: Protection with Artificial Intelligence
The **Precision AI** technology offered by Palo Alto Networks combines generative AI with cyber defense to:
- Detect attacks in real-time with higher accuracy.
- Provide security analysts with natural language querying and analysis capabilities (Cortex Copilot).
