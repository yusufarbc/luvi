## Introduction: Security Without Boundaries
Palo Alto Networks takes a platform-based approach to cybersecurity, closing the gaps created by siloed solutions. Through **Strata, Prisma, and Cortex**, Luvi Bilişim redefines your cyber defense with the power of AI and automation.

---

## 1. Strata™: The Foundation of Network Security (NGFW)
Protect your network against known and unknown threats with the world's first ML-Powered Next-Generation Firewalls.

- **PAN-OS®:** Consistent policy management across all form factors (Physical, Virtual, Container).
- **App-ID™:** Identifies and controls applications based on content, not just ports.
- **WildFire®:** Cloud-delivered malware analysis that shares threat intelligence globally in seconds.

---

## 2. Prisma™ Cloud: Cloud-Native Security (CNAPP)
Secure your data and applications across all cloud environments (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).

- **Prisma Cloud:** Full visibility from code to runtime, protecting your entire cloud-native stack.
- **Prisma Access (SASE):** Provides hybrid workers with secure, fast "Zero Trust" connectivity from anywhere.
- **Prisma SaaS:** Prevents data loss and ensures compliance across cloud-based applications (Office 365, Salesforce).

---

## 3. Cortex™: Autonomous Security Operations (XDR)
Accelerate your incident response and automate manual security processes.

- **Cortex XDR:** Unifies network, endpoint, and cloud data to automatically analyze the root cause of attacks.
- **Cortex XSOAR:** Orchestrates your security operations with hundreds of integrations and ready-to-use "Playbooks."
- **Cortex XPANSE:** Continuously scans your external attack surface to identify and remediate unknown risks.
