# Mobile Device Management (MDM) Consulting

We align your employees' mobile devices with your corporate security standards.

### Service Details:
- **System Integration**: Inclusion of MDM solutions into your existing infrastructure.
- **Scope Definition**: Design of device policies and access authorizations.
- **Management Awareness**: Reporting processes and strategic decision support.
- **Risk-Based Security**: Continuous auditing and improvement with the PDCA model.
