In the digital age, protecting data is not just a requirement, but the foundation of corporate sustainability.

- **Data Classification**: Labeling and managing data according to its sensitivity.
- **Data Loss Prevention (DLP)**: Preventing unauthorized leakage of critical data.
- **Access Management**: Ensuring only authorized people can reach relevant data.
