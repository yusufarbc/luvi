# Phishing Simulation & Social Engineering

More than 90% of cyber attacks are caused by human error. We transform your employees into cybersecurity defenders who recognize and block these attacks.

### Our Service Scope:
- **Phishing Simulations**: Measuring employee alertness with realistic scenarios.
- **Social Engineering Tests**: Testing corporate security via phone, email, and physical methods.
- **USB Drop Tests**: Awareness measurement against unknown devices.
- **Detailed Reporting**: Department-based risk analysis and identification of training needs.

### Why is it Important?
No matter how strong technological measures are, one click can paralyze all systems. We minimize this risk with our social engineering tests.
