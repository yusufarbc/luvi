# Penetration Testing

We analyze institutions' digital assets through an attacker's eyes, detecting and reporting potential security vulnerabilities.

### Our Service Areas:
- **Web Application Pentesting**: Deep analysis according to OWASP standards.
- **Network Infrastructure Pentesting**: Measuring the security level of internal and external networks.
- **Mobile Application Pentesting**: Security of your iOS and Android applications.
- **Social Engineering Tests**: Measuring employee awareness.
- **Physical Security Tests**: Office and data center entry security.

### Our Testing Processes:
1. **Planning and Preparation**: Defining the scope and targets.
2. **Reconnaissance**: Passive and active information gathering about the target system.
3. **Vulnerability Analysis**: Risk scoring of detected vulnerabilities.
