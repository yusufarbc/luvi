Server virtualization and data backup are two inseparable parts of modern IT infrastructure.

### Benefits:
- **Resource Optimization**: Reducing operational costs by decreasing the number of physical servers.
- **Zero Data Loss**: Automated and redundant data storage systems.
- **Fast Recovery**: Restoring systems in minutes in case of any failure.
- **Flexibility**: Hybrid management of cloud and local infrastructures.
