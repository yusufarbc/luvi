KVKK (Personal Data Protection Law) compliance becomes sustainable with employee awareness, not just technical measures.

### Training Content:
- **Basic Concepts**: Concepts of personal data, sensitive personal data, data controller, and data processor.
- **Legal Responsibilities**: Administrative and criminal sanctions under the law.
- **Data Security Discipline**: Methods for protecting data in daily operations.
- **Rights and Notifications**: Rights of data subjects and actions to be taken in case of breach.
