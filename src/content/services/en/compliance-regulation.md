In the complex world of regulations, we secure your organization by bringing together legal and technical compliance.

### KVKK & Data Privacy Compliance
We conduct legal and technical analysis for full compliance with the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) in Turkey, and align your processes with the law.

### ISO Certification Consultancy
We manage preparation and audit processes for international standards such as ISO 27001 (Information Security), ISO 22301 (Business Continuity), and ISO 20000 (Service Management).

### IT Audit & GRC
We audit your systems within the framework of IT Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), and report your compliance with COBIT and similar global standards.

### Industrial & Critical Infrastructure Security
By ensuring compliance with critical regulations such as ISA/IEC 62443 and the Government Security Guide, we certify the security of your industrial systems (OT/ICS).
